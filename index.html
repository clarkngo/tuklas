About Tuklas

Bridging knowledge and community through mentorship

Why "Tuklas"?

"Tuklas" means discover in Filipino — capturing the spirit of a quest for new knowledge. Our name echoes Magellan's expedition reaching the Philippines, symbolizing exploration and uncovering new frontiers.

Our Logo

The logo draws from the Tinikling — a traditional Filipino dance requiring coordination, adaptability, and rhythm. These same qualities define a great learning journey.

The Problem We Solve

There is a gap between talented CityU graduate students with specialized skills and the communities that could benefit from their knowledge — startups, nonprofits, retirees, and career switchers.

Our Solution

A platform to book one-on-one sessions or group workshops with CityU students and faculty. An AI-powered recommendation engine helps you find the session that best fits your goals.
